

Person Interviewed: Ken  Kissenger

Profession: Photographer, Videographer, and Media Content Creator

I spoke with Kissenger to understand his requirements for a professional website. He explained that he provides photography, videography, live streaming, and social media content creation services for events, businesses, churches, and organizations.

His target audience includes event organizers, businesses, churches, NGOs, and individuals looking for professional media coverage. He wanted the website to present a professional image, showcase his work, and make it easy for potential clients to contact him.

The most important information to display includes:

* A brief introduction about the company 
* Services offered
* A portfolio/gallery of previous work
* Contact information
* Social media links

Kissenger preferred a modern and clean design with a black, white, and grey scheme. He also requested a large banner image that immediately communicates
 his profession as a photographer and videographer.

The primary goal of the website is to encourage visitors to view his portfolio and contact him for bookings and inquiries.
